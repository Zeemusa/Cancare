Cancer is not just one disease but a group of diseases that occcur when abnormal cells grow uncontrollable, forming a mass turmor. Those cells can spread to other parts of the body, making cancer a completetly and sometimes an unpredictable disease. Ther are over 100 types of cancers, each affecting different organs or systems in the body.
